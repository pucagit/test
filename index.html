Influencer Marketing - Global Glory

Context

Founded in Vietnam in 2023, Global Glory specializes in personal care products. The brand focuses on Shopee and TikTok Shop to boost product visibility and sales. One of its key strengths is the young and agile team, which is skilled in spotting trends, creating engaging content, and running performance advertising.

Action

I began by identifying KOCs whose content aligned with our product category and crafting a single, memorable hook for each product.

Coordinating all KOCs to post the hook at the same time made the brand instantly recognizable to every viewer.
